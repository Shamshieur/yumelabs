About YumeLabs

Welcome to the Lab where dreams take shape

Our Story

Born in 2021, we started humbly on Etsy with one big goal: to make the world of VTubing more accessible, expressive, and fun. Today, we're crafting a vibrant space full of creative digital assets for indie VTubers who want to stand out without breaking the bank. Now in 2025, we decided to launch our own website.

Why "YumeLabs"?

"Yume" (夢) means dream in Japanese, and this is our dream lab—where identities are experimented with, characters evolve, and aesthetics come to life. Our logo? A cute anime head in a chemistry flask. It's not just adorable—it's a symbol of what we do: persona experimentation at its finest.

What We Do

We design and sell digital goodies that help VTubers express who they really are. Our asset library includes:

✧Stream Overlays

Animated scenes and overlays that make your stream stand out with professional polish.

✧Stage Elements

Backgrounds, mic art, gaming chair accessories and other virtual stage enhancers.

✧Character Add-ons

Personality-driven elements that help your VTuber persona shine.

And more quirky, stylish designs you didn't know you needed. If it enhances your virtual stage, it probably belongs in YumeLabs.

Our Mission

We believe everyone deserves the chance to build a unique VTuber identity—no matter the budget. That's why we focus on:

✧Affordable Assets

Quality designs at prices that don't require sponsorships or donations.

✧Stylized Variety

Wide range of themes and vibes to match different personalities.

✧Instant Delivery

Digital downloads mean you're not waiting forever to go live.

Who's Behind the Curtain?

We're a small studio (okay, it's mostly a one-human show) using smart tools—including AI—to create bold designs. When needed, we collaborate with artists to add extra polish to our products. It's all part of the experiment.

The Future of YumeLabs

In the next few years, we're evolving into something bigger: a platform for artists and VTubers to connect, collaborate, and co-create. Think of it as a hybrid between a digital store and a creative playground—a space where the community fuels the next wave of expression.
